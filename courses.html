Programs Offered

Comprehensive training programs designed to prepare you for successful careers in the hospitality industry

Diploma in Hospitality Management

Duration: 1 Year Eligibility: 10+2 or equivalent

Course Overview

This comprehensive program covers all aspects of hotel operations including front office, housekeeping, food production, and food & beverage service. Students gain practical experience through industry internships and hands-on training in our state-of-the-art facilities.

Key Subjects

  • Front Office Operations
  • Housekeeping Management
  • Food Production Techniques
  • Food & Beverage Service
  • Hotel Accounting
  • Hospitality Marketing
  • Communication Skills

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers as Front Office Executives, Housekeeping Supervisors, F&B Service Managers, Kitchen Supervisors, and various other roles in hotels, resorts, cruise lines, and restaurants.

Diploma in Aviation

Duration: 1 Year Eligibility: 10+2 or equivalent

Key Subjects

  • Cabin Crew Operations
  • Aviation Safety & Emergency Procedures
  • Passenger Handling
  • In-flight Service
  • Grooming & Personality Development
  • Aviation Industry Knowledge

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers as Cabin Crew, Ground Staff, Customer Service Executives, and Passenger Service Agents with airlines and airports.
