Our Facilities

Explore our state-of-the-art training facilities designed to provide hands-on experience

World-Class Training Facilities

At Parun Institute of Hotel Management, we believe in providing our students with the best learning environment. Our state-of-the-art facilities are designed to simulate real-world hospitality settings, allowing students to gain practical experience alongside theoretical knowledge.

Each of our specialized labs is equipped with industry-standard equipment and tools, ensuring our students are well-prepared for their future careers in the hospitality industry.

Food Production Lab

Our Food Production Lab is equipped with modern kitchen equipment and tools that allow students to learn various cooking techniques and cuisines. The lab features:

  • Professional cooking ranges and ovens
  • Bakery and confectionery section
  • Cold kitchen area
  • Specialized equipment for international cuisines
  • Food storage and preparation areas
  • Demonstration area with audio-visual facilities

Students get hands-on experience in preparing various dishes under the guidance of experienced chefs and instructors.

F&B Service Lab

Our Food & Beverage Service Lab simulates a real restaurant environment where students learn various service techniques and protocols. The lab features:

  • Fine dining restaurant setup
  • Casual dining area
  • Banquet setup section
  • Various types of tableware, glassware, and cutlery
  • Service stations and sideboards
  • POS systems for billing and order management

Students practice different service styles including French, American, Russian, and buffet service in this lab.

Bar Tending Lab

Our specialized Bar Tending Lab is designed to train students in the art and science of mixology and bar operations. The lab features:

  • Fully equipped bar counter
  • Wide range of glassware
  • Bar tools and equipment
  • Mock spirits and liqueurs for practice
  • Flair bartending practice area
  • Bar POS system

Students learn cocktail preparation, flair bartending, bar management, and customer service skills in this lab.

Housekeeping Lab

Our Housekeeping Lab provides students with practical training in hotel room maintenance and cleaning procedures. The lab features:

  • Model hotel rooms with different bed types
  • Bathroom fixtures and amenities
  • Housekeeping equipment and supplies
  • Linen room setup
  • Laundry operation area
  • Cleaning chemical storage and handling area

Students learn bed making, room cleaning, turndown service, and inventory management in this lab.

Front Office Lab

Our Front Office Lab simulates a hotel reception area where students learn guest handling and front desk operations. The lab features:

  • Reception desk with check-in/check-out counters
  • Concierge and bell desk area
  • Property Management System (PMS)
  • Reservation systems
  • Key card systems
  • Telephone exchange setup

Students practice guest registration, reservation management, billing, and guest relations in this lab.
